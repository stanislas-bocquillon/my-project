What is a manatee ?

Manatees, the sea cows

Manatees are large, gentle sea creatures that are known for their docile nature and friendly interactions with humans. They are herbivores and can be found in warm, shallow waters along the coasts of Florida, the Caribbean, and parts of South America. Due to habitat loss and hunting, manatees are considered a vulnerable species, and conservation efforts are crucial for their survival.

To protect manatees, it is important to reduce boat strikes, preserve their habitat, educate the public about their importance, and support conservation efforts. By taking these steps, we can help ensure the survival of these gentle sea creatures.
